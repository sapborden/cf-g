STEF BORDEN

My name is Stef. I am a 4th year undergraduate student at the University of St Andrews, studying for a BSc in Mathematics & Philosophy.


In Mathematics, I am currently studying Complex Analysis, Mathematical Computing and I am writing my dissertation on the history of proving impossibility in mathematics, with a focus on the 19th century.


In Philosophy, I am currently taking a class on Paradoxes and writing on the nature of vagueness.


The idea for this website came from a note that I have on my phone. It is a long note filled with quotes that I scribble down while reading books, articles, blogs etc. I decided to put all these quotes in one place.
